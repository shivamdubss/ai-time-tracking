How It Works

Three steps. Zero effort.

1

Open Donna and work

Donna runs in the background while you draft motions, research case law, and handle client calls. It observes your active window and captures screenshots for AI context.

2

AI generates your timesheet

When you stop tracking, Donna sends your activity data to Claude AI, which produces categorized billing entries with professional narratives. All raw data is immediately destroyed.

3

Review, edit, export

Review AI-generated entries, adjust narratives if needed, assign to matters, and export a clean timesheet. Donna handles the paperwork so you can handle the cases.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What does Donna actually track?
Donna monitors your active window title every 3 seconds and captures periodic screenshots (every 30 seconds by default). Both are used by AI to generate billing narratives, then permanently deleted. Only the AI-generated summaries are kept.
Is my client data sent to the cloud?
Activity data is sent only to the Anthropic API via an encrypted TLS connection for AI summarization. Anthropic does not store or train on this data. No screenshots, window logs, or client information are stored on any external server. All raw data is permanently deleted from your device immediately after processing.
What AI model does Donna use?
Donna uses Claude by Anthropic. Your activity data is sent via an encrypted API call, processed into billing narratives, and never stored or used for model training. You provide your own Anthropic API key, so you have full control over the AI processing.
What platforms does Donna support?
Donna runs as a native desktop application on macOS and Windows. It installs locally on your machine and runs entirely on your device. There is no web version or mobile app — by design, this ensures client data never leaves your computer.
How does billing work?
Time displays in 0.1-hour (6-minute) increments, the legal industry standard since the 1970s. Rounding is configurable: round-up (default), round-down, or round-nearest. You can set hourly rates at the default, per-client, or per-matter level. Billable values are calculated automatically.
Can I edit AI-generated narratives?
Yes. Every narrative can be edited after generation. You can also adjust time entries, change activity categories, reassign entries to different matters, and mark entries as billable or non-billable. Donna gives you a strong starting point, not a locked-in result.
How do I export my timesheets?
One-click CSV export from the Timesheet view. You can filter by date range, client, and matter before exporting. The CSV includes all fields needed for billing: date, client, matter, hours, rate, billable value, category, and narrative.
Is Donna free?
Donna is free during early access. No credit card required. You will need your own Anthropic API key for AI summarization, which typically costs a few cents per session summary.
